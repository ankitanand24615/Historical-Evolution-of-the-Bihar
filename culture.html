5. Spiritual Heritage

Bihar is the birthplace of both Buddhism and Jainism. Lord Buddha attained enlightenment in Bodh Gaya, and Lord Mahavira preached in Vaishali. These sacred places attract pilgrims and peace seekers from around the world.
