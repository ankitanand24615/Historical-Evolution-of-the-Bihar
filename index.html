Welcome

Bihar has been one of the most influential regions in India’s history — the birthplace of empires, religions, and revolutions. This website takes you on a journey through Bihar’s transformation from the ancient kingdom of Magadha to the modern state we know today.

Explore timelines, historic photos, maps, and archives below.

Discover the rise of Magadha, the Mauryan Empire, and centers of learning like Nalanda and Vikramshila.

Colonial & Pre-Independence

Learn how Bihar played a vital role in India’s struggle for freedom, from the 1857 revolt to Gandhi’s Champaran Satyagraha.

Post-Independence Bihar

Explore the political, social, and economic transformation of Bihar since 1947, including the formation of Jharkhand.
